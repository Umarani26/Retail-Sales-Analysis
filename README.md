# Retail Sales Analysis: Customer Behavior and Revenue Insights

## Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data using Python to gain insights into customer purchasing behavior, product category performance, monthly revenue trends, and top customers. The analysis includes data cleaning, aggregation, and visualization to help understand sales performance and support business decision-making.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall retail sales performance.
- Identify revenue generated by each product category.
- Examine monthly revenue trends.
- Discover the top customers based on total revenue.
- Visualize key business insights using charts.

## Features

- Data preprocessing and cleaning
- Revenue analysis by product category
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Top 10 customers by revenue
- Data visualization with bar charts
- Business insights and recommendations

## Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue by Product Category
The analysis compares total revenue across different product categories to identify the highest-performing products.

### 2. Monthly Revenue Trend
Monthly revenue is analyzed to understand sales patterns, seasonal variations, and business performance over time.

### 3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
Customers are ranked based on total revenue generated, helping identify high-value customers for retention and loyalty programs.

### 4. Revenue by Age Group
This analysis compares total revenue across different age groups to highlight which segments contribute the most and to identify the highest-performing products

### 5. Revenue Share by Gender
This analysis breaks down total revenue contributions by gender, showing the proportion of revenue generated by male and female.

### 6. Average Transaction Value by Month
Tracks the average amount spent per transaction each month. This helps identify seasonal spending patterns and monitor customer purchasing behavior.

### 7. Quantity Sold by Product Category
Measures the total units sold within each product category. This highlights top‑selling categories and helps guide inventory and marketing strategies.

### 8. Day-of-Week Sales Pattern
Analyzes sales distribution across each day of the week. This reveals peak shopping days and helps optimize promotions and staffing.

## Key Insights

- Clothing generated the highest overall revenue.
- Electronics performed nearly as well as Electronics.
- Beauty products generated comparatively lower revenue.
- Revenue fluctuated across different months, indicating seasonal sales patterns.

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Improve promotional strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Reward loyal customers through discounts and loyalty programs.
- Use monthly sales trends for inventory planning and marketing campaigns.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Python can be used to analyze retail sales data and generate meaningful business insights. By exploring customer behavior, product performance, and revenue trends, the analysis supports data-driven decision-making and provides a foundation for future predictive analytics projects.
